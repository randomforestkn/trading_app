# Store Listing Draft

## App name

ClearTrade

## Short description

Paper trading workspace for learning, journaling, and strategy review.

## Long description

ClearTrade is a local-first paper trading app for practicing market workflows
without real money. It combines a premium dark trading interface with portfolio
tracking, simulated market data, journaling, analytics, options strategy
simulation, and backup/restore tools.

The app is designed for education and workflow rehearsal, not live brokerage
execution. Market prices may be simulated unless a remote market provider is
configured. Options tools are calculators and trackers, not brokerage order
routers.

### Feature highlights

- Paper trading portfolio with cash, positions, and activity history
- Market watchlist and asset detail views
- Strategy simulator for covered calls, cash-secured puts, and the wheel
- Options portfolio and income tracking
- Trading journal with notes, moods, and lessons learned
- Analytics, insights, exports, and backup / restore tools
- Local-first storage with optional provider-ready boundaries

## Disclaimers

- Paper trading only - no real money involved.
- Not investment advice.
- No brokerage execution is provided.
- Market data may be simulated or provider-limited depending on configuration.
- Options tools are educational calculators only.

## Privacy / local-first positioning

- User data is stored locally by default.
- Export and restore are manual, user-controlled actions.
- Remote providers are optional and off by default.
